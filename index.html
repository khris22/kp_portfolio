Hello! I'm Khris.

I’ve long been driven by love of learning and doing something that challenges me and pushing myself to the edge of my abilities as I always want to prove that I can do things that I set my mind into. After graduating from the university, I decided to move to Japan to study Japanese and at that time I was also offered a teaching position. I loved studying the language, the culture, and teaching so much that I decided to stay for 8 years!

Having a job in education for more than 10 years now, the feeling has become stagnant and I needed a change. A change that can satisfy my love of learning and that can push me out of my comfort zone and challenge me, hence, I started coding. It was a lot of work balancing my studies, a full-time job, and my personal life but with good time management I made it work. I took a leap and it was one of the scariest decisions I have made but it has been well worth it.

While I was studying, I realized that being a software engineer is like being in education, in that you will always have to learn. There will always be something new that will come up and you have to adapt. I finally felt that spark of curiosity igniting again; giving me the motivation to work harder. Dealing with different languages and different behaviors of the app you’re making is a lot like being in a classroom of kids. There are so many approaches you can take, so many solutions you can think of, and so many challenging and learning experiences.

I’m currently looking for a software engineering role that will help me continue to learn and challenge myself. I’m looking forward to this new chapter that will give me an opportunity to hone my skills and continue the love of coding that has been ignited.
